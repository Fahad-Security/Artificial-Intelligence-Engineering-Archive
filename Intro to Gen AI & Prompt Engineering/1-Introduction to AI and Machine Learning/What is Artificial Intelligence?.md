# 🧠 What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

Artificial Intelligence is:

The simulation of human intelligence inside machines (programs or systems) so they can think, learn, and make decisions.

**In simple terms:**
Instead of humans analyzing and deciding, machines learn from data and start predicting and deciding on their own.

---

# ⚙️ How AI Works (Core Idea)

Any AI system goes through three main stages:

## 1. Data Collection
It gathers massive amounts of data (images, text, numbers, etc.).

## 2. Learning
It looks for:
- Patterns  
- Relationships  
- Repeated events  

## 3. Prediction or Decision
After learning, it starts:
- Predicting outcomes  
- Classifying data  
- Generating new content  

---

# 🧩 Key Capabilities of AI

- 📚 Learning: from data  
- 🧠 Reasoning: choosing the right solution  
- 🔁 Self-correction: improving over time  
- 🎨 Creativity: generating text, images, and ideas  

---

# 🔥 AI vs ML vs Deep Learning

## 🤖 Artificial Intelligence (AI)
The big umbrella: any system that mimics human intelligence.

## 📊 Machine Learning (ML)
A subset of AI: learns from data without explicit programming.

## 🧬 Deep Learning (DL)
A subset of ML: uses neural networks inspired by the human brain.

**Example:**
- AI = Smart car (full system)  
- ML = Driving system  
- DL = Vision/brain of the system  

---

# 🧠 Types of AI

## 1. Narrow AI (Weak AI)
Performs only one task:
- ChatGPT  
- Siri  
- Netflix recommendations  

## 2. General AI (AGI) ❌ Not existing yet
Can think like a human in any task.

## 3. Other Categories
- Reactive machines (no memory)  
- Limited memory (self-driving cars)  
- Theory of mind (future concept)  
- Self-aware AI (science fiction)
