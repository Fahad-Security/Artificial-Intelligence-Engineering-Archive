# 🧠 The Main Idea of the Text

The story (John + Netflix + Siri + Tesla) is meant to explain one simple idea:

👉 Artificial Intelligence is already all around you every day without you noticing it.

For example:

- Netflix suggests movies 🎬  
- Siri answers questions 🎤  
- Tesla drives cars 🚗  

All of them are powered by AI.

---

# 🤖 What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

AI is:

Making computers or machines think and act like humans.

But very important:
✔ It is NOT real human intelligence  
✔ It is “learning from data + executing decisions”

---

# 📜 Who invented the term?

John McCarthy  
Year: 1956

But real progress came later due to:

- The need for powerful computing  
- Huge amounts of data  

---

# 🧩 Types of Artificial Intelligence

## 1️⃣ Narrow AI (ANI / Weak AI)

This is what we use today.

It performs only specific tasks like:

- Netflix (movie recommendations)  
- Siri (voice assistant)  
- Chatbots  

📌 It does NOT understand everything — only its specific task.

---

## 2️⃣ General AI (AGI)

A human-like intelligence.

- Can understand and learn anything  
- Thinks like a human  

⚠️ It does NOT fully exist yet.

Some models like GPT are considered “a step toward it,” but they are not truly human-level intelligence.

---

## 3️⃣ Super AI (ASI)

- More intelligent than humans  
- Would outperform humans in everything  

💡 This exists only in science fiction for now.

---

# 🔗 Relationship Between AI, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning

## 📊 Machine Learning (ML)

A subset of AI.

It learns from data without explicit programming.

Examples:
- Spam detection  
- Disease diagnosis  

---

## 🧬 Deep Learning (DL)

A subset of Machine Learning.

It uses “neural networks” inspired by the human brain.

More powerful and complex.

📌 Used in:
- Face recognition 👤  
- Voice recognition 🎤  
- Self-driving cars 🚗  

---

## 🗣️ NLP (Natural Language Processing)

Understanding human language.

- Translation  
- Chatbots  
- Text understanding  

📌 Examples:
- ChatGPT  
- Siri
